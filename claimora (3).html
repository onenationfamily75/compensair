❓ FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting your claim.

You are entitled to receive 75% of your original ticket price for eligible claims. For example, if your ticket cost $400, your refund would be $300. You can use our built-in refund calculator in the claim form to see your exact estimate before submitting.
You may be eligible if: your flight was delayed 3+ hours, cancelled with less than 14 days notice, you were denied boarding due to overbooking, or your baggage was lost/damaged. Disruptions caused by extraordinary circumstances (severe weather, strikes) generally exempt airlines.
Our team responds within 24–48 business hours with your eligibility assessment. The full airline claims process takes 4–12 weeks depending on airline cooperation and jurisdiction.
A one-time upfront service fee of 25% of your ticket price is charged before your claim is processed. For example, a $400 ticket = $100 service fee. This is non-refundable and covers our manual review and claim guidance work.
Required: boarding pass or e-ticket, booking confirmation, and payment receipt for the service fee. Helpful extras: airline emails about the disruption, hotel/meal receipts, any correspondence with the airline.

📋 Full Process Guide

You are entitled to 75% of your ticket price as a refund. Service fee must be paid before submitting.
1

Check Eligibility

Your flight must have been delayed 3+ hours, cancelled under 14 days notice, denied boarding, or baggage lost/damaged. Extraordinary circumstances may disqualify claims.

2

Calculate Your 75% Refund

Enter your ticket price in our refund calculator. Your refund = 75% of your ticket price. Example: $400 ticket → $300 refund. Select your currency for accurate calculation.

3

Pay Service Fee

Fee = 25% of your ticket price (e.g. $400 ticket → $100 fee). Use our built-in calculator in the claim form to see your exact amount. Pay via bank transfer — our team will provide payment details upon request. Use your name + flight number as the reference and save your receipt.

4

Email Your Claim

Email refundfly@gmail.com with: full name, email, phone, airline, flight number, date, airports, reason, ticket price. Attach: boarding pass + payment receipt (both required).

5

Await Response (24–48h)

Our team responds within 24–48 business hours with your full eligibility verdict, confirmed refund amount (75% of your ticket), and next steps.
